Home

Welcome to my hobby site! Flying drones and taking aerial pictures is a hobby of mine. I mainly take pictures of landscapes but am open to everything else.

Here, I'll show some of the things I have done. From the best pictures I have taken to the best places to take pictures and why this hobby is so rewarding.

About Me

I am a frequent drone pilot and photographer with about 4 years of experience. This started as I have always found drones interesting and love the outdoors.

I mainly take photos of landscapes but am open to anything else. I have even taken pictures of houses to help a realtor.

This hobby doesn't require much to get started, just a passion for it! Take me, for example: I don't have the most advanced drone, but I'm still able to fully enjoy flying and take amazing photos.

Looking towards the future I plan on getting a drone certification in order to be able to fly in more locations and to sell my photos.

Best Times

Any time is a great time to fly a drone! Whether it's during the spring, summer, fall or winter. Each season and time of day offers unique opportunities for capturing stunning aerial shots!

Just be careful when flying on top of mountains due to high winds and always watch out for rain as it can break the drone.

Best Locations

Some of my favorite locations for drone photography are coastlines, urban skylines, lakes and mountains. Open fields and forests also offer breathtaking aerial views.

Be sure to follow local laws and regulations regarding drone flights in these areas.

How

Getting started with drones is easier than ever! Start with a beginner-friendly model, take time to learn flight controls, and always practice safety measures.

From experience DJI drones are user friendly and have models for all budgets and skill levels.

Why

Drone flying combines technology with creativity. It creates unique perspectives of the world that are impossible to achieve with regular photography.

This hobby also promotes learning, exploration, and the joy of capturing breathtaking visuals from above.
