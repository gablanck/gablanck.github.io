Website Evaluations

Evaluating websites based on the CRAP principles of design.

Good Website

Apple

Apple's website is known for its clean, modern design and easy navigation. It is visually appealing, easy to use, and well-organized.

  • Contrast: Apple uses high contrast between text and background for easy readability, with visually prominent calls to action.
  • Repetition: Consistent color scheme, font choices, and layout across the site.
  • Alignment: All content is aligned properly, creating a clean and organized layout.
  • Proximity: Related items are grouped together, making it easy to understand and navigate.

Bad Website

Craigslist

Craigslist has a dated design with poor use of contrast and alignment. The site feels cluttered and unorganized.

  • Contrast: The site uses poor contrast, making text difficult to read, especially with low contrast between links and background.
  • Repetition: The layout is inconsistent, with varying fonts and sizes that feel chaotic.
  • Alignment: The elements on the site are misaligned, giving it an unorganized and cluttered appearance.
  • Proximity: Related items are not grouped together well, making the site difficult to navigate.
